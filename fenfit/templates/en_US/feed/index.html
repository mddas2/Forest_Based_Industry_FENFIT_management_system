]]> Federation of Forest Based Industry and Trade, Nepal (FenFIT) is a national not for-profit, non-political, self-governed and professional organization established in 1990 to promote responsible forest based industry and trade in Nepal. With the establishment of multi-parties system in Nepal, FenFIT re-structured its organization in 2009.  It has been established which is an umbrella organization of timber and non-timber forests products industries and traders in Nepal.

It is a platform for forest traders and timber industries and work to find solutions to improve forest management practices as well.FenFIT works to ensure the permanent existence of forest areas through responsible forest utilization and Marketing .
